Short Description
Poodles

The Poodle is one of dogdom's real aristocrats and comes in Standard, Miniature, or Toy sizes, as well as in black, white, or apricot colors. There is an exquisite athlete and friend for all occasions and seasons hiding behind the low-allergen, curly coat.
Siberian Huskys

A medium-sized, compact sled dog with exceptional endurance and a thick coat known as the Siberian Husky was bred to operate in packs and pull modest loads through wide, icy terrain at a moderate speed.
Golden Retrievers

The Golden Retriever, a vivacious Scottish gundog with exceptional beauty, is one of the most popular dog breeds in America.
